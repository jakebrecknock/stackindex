About CodeCove

Our Mission

CodeCove is a vibrant community for developers who "vibe code" – creating amazing projects through intuition and joy. Share your creations, discover inspiration, and connect with fellow makers.

How It Works

Share your project with a description, link, and up to 5 tags. Upvote projects you love, favorite them, comment with feedback, and build your portfolio in a supportive environment.

Community Guidelines

Be kind, give constructive feedback, respect others' work, and have fun! Projects of all skill levels are welcome – we're all here to grow together.
